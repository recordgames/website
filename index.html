About Us

Record Games is a UK games studio building games around musical self-expression. Our first title, Starstruck Galaxy, combines rhythm-game accessibility with original music creation, career progression, and live-ops foundations to help players become musicians through play.

Our team combines deep tech, games, music education, audio, data, live-ops, marketing, and art experience, with backgrounds across Sumo Digital, Creative Assembly, Yousician, 505 Games, Codemasters, and Paradromics.
